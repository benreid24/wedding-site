Information

Everything You Need To Know

When And Where

Both the Ceremony and the Reception will be taking place at Hidden Valley Animal Adventure, located at 2887 Royce Rd, Varysburg, NY 14167. The Ceremony starts at 5:00PM. Please arrive at least 15 minutes early. The ceremony will be taking place at the outdoor gazebo overlooking the pond. Seating for all will be provided. We are hiring a professional photographer and will have plenty of pictures to show or give to you at a later date, so please relax and enjoy the ceremony with your cameras put away.

Dress Code

We would like all our guests to be comfortable and to be ready to partake in outdoor activities if they desire to. Therefore, nice but casual dress is allowed, including shorts with button-ups/polo shirts and sundresses. Please no pajama pants, sweats, or lounge wear. However, if you feel like being more dressed up that is acceptable as well.

Local Accommodations

If you are coming in from out of town or would like a place to stay the night of the wedding we will be contacting some local hotels to reserve a block of rooms. We will update here with information. We will also be chartering a bus to provide transportation between the hotel and the venue. Transportation may also be provided to other locations if there are enough guests traveling to them.

Animal Adventure

There is an option to partake in the Safari Tour offered at Hidden Valley. This tour is 30 minutes long will take place during the first half of cocktail hour promptly after the ceremony. On the tour you will ride on a trolley through the grounds of Hidden Valley to view the animals up close. You will be given a cup of feed with which you can feed the various animals present, including but not limited to zebras, buffalo, and antelope. Guests that sign up for the tour will also be allowed one visit to the Small Animal Zoo located next to the Reception Lodge. Only guests that sign up for the Safari Tour on their RSVP will be allowed to participate; therefore, please sign up if you are interested.

Menu

Dinner will be plated. Guests may choose from the options below. Please indicate how many of each meal you would like on your RSVP. The number of meals requested must match the number of people on your RSVP. An open bar will be provided during cocktail hour and after the dinner hour for all guests age 21 and older. Please contact us if you have any special dietary restrictions.

Meals With Meat
  • Petite Filet Mignon with Demi Glace and Chicken Piccata, Classic Mashed Potatoes
  • Sliced Roast Tenderloin of Beef with Demi Glace and Maryland Crab Cake with Spicy Remoulade, Roasted Red Potatoes
  • Chicken Fingers (Age 12 and under only)
Vegetarian Meals
  • Vegetable Stir Fry with Teriyaki Sauce over Jasmine Rice
  • Roasted Vegetable Wellington, Fresh Seasonal Vegetables, Puff Pastry & Sundried Tomato Basil Pesto

Gifts

There is no expectation of gifts, however if you would still like to give us anything please limit it to monetary gifts only. Please also consider donating to a charity on our behalf instead as the greatest gift is your presence.
